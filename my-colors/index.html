Colors

By: Amanda Rogers

This is a triadic color scheme. It consists of three different colors that are evenly spaced throughout the color wheel. I am using it to give a vibrant and playful feel to the web page. The red represents passion and love, the yellow represents naiveness and an idyllic state, and he blue represents calmness and a cerebral state. A good triadic color scheme is hard to achieve, but it can put out a lot of fun emotions. A good triadic color scheme usually consist of one main color and two accent colors. Mine does not follow this pattern, as I used two main colors and one accent color. I did elect to make the yellow more of an accent color, because I thought website would appear disorganized if all of the colors were bright. The tone of the color scheme can be changed to be more solemn by making the colors more muted.
