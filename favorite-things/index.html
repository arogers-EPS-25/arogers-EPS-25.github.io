Amanda's Favorite Thing About School

By: Amanda Rogers

Big Question: What is your favorite thing about school?

I enjoy many things about EPS.

I like:

  1. The classes
  2. The teachers
  3. The strong community
  4. The food
  5. My friends
  6. The Tali 3rd floor (specifically the section outside Ms. McKinney's office)
  7. The square couches in Tali
  8. Mr. Sternitzky (he is awsome)
  9. And so much more

My favorite thing about school though are the EBC trips. I went to Catalina Island off the coast of LA for a 6 day backpacking trip and absolutely loved it. I saw so many new animals, met new people, strengthened my relationships with my teachers and friends, as well as I got to go for a sunrise hike. EBC week is my favorite week out of the entire year, and they keep getting better. I hope to continue to do more outdoor trip (hopefully backpacking) in different parts of the world.
